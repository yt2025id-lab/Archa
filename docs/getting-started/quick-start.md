# Quick Start

Get started with Archa in 5 minutes!

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, you'll need:
- A Web3 wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, or Coinbase Wallet)
- Some MNT tokens for gas fees (very small amount needed)
- USDC tokens for deposits and collateral

## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

1. Visit [arisanonchain.vercel.app](https://arisanonchain.vercel.app)
2. Click the "Connect Wallet" button in the top right
3. Choose your preferred wallet provider
4. Approve the connection request in your wallet

{% hint style="info" %}
Make sure you're connected to **Mantle Sepolia Testnet** (Chain ID: 5003) for testing, or Mantle Mainnet for production.
{% endhint %}

## Step 2: Get Test Tokens (Testnet Only)

For testing on Mantle Sepolia:

1. Get test MNT from the [Mantle Faucet](https://faucet.sepolia.mantle.xyz)
2. Get test USDC from our app - click "Get Test USDC" in the footer

## Step 3: Choose a Pool

1. Navigate to the "Pools" page
2. Browse available pools with different:
   - Monthly deposit amounts (10/50/100 USDC)
   - Number of participants (5/10/20 people)
   - Collateral requirements
3. Click on a pool to view details

## Step 4: Join the Pool

1. On the pool detail page, click "Join Pool"
2. First, approve USDC spending (one-time)
3. Then, deposit your collateral
4. You're now a participant!

## Step 5: Make Monthly Deposits

Each month (or as scheduled):
1. Visit your pool's page
2. Click "Deposit"
3. Confirm the transaction
4. The AI will automatically invest your deposit
