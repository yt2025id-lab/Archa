## Mission

> "Preserving Indonesia's arisan tradition with blockchain technology, making it safer, more transparent, and more profitable for hundreds of millions of Indonesians."

Archa exists to solve the fundamental problem in traditional arisan: **trust**. With smart contracts, there's no more worry about participants running away or organizers cheating.

## Vision

To become the largest onchain arisan platform in the world, starting from Indonesia - the country with the strongest arisan tradition and the 7th largest crypto population in the world.

## Arisan Transformation

| Aspect | Old Arisan | New Arisan (Archa) |
|--------|------------|-------------------|
| **Trust** | Based on personal trust | Trustless via smart contract |
| **Run-away Risk** | High | None (collateral system) |
| **Yield** | 0% (idle funds) | 5-15% APY (AI optimized) |
| **Transparency** | Manual, easy to manipulate | 100% on-chain |
| **Reach** | Close friends/family | Anyone in the world |
| **Record-keeping** | Books/spreadsheets | Immutable blockchain |

## Core Values

### 1. Tradition
We respect and preserve the gotong royong (mutual cooperation) essence of traditional arisan. Archa doesn't replace arisan - it takes it to the next level.

### 2. Trust
Smart contracts guarantee every rule is executed without exception. There's no room for manipulation or fraud.

### 3. Profit
Collected funds should never sit idle. AI Yield Optimizer ensures every dollar works to generate passive income.

### 4. Inclusivity
Anyone can join Archa arisan, no need to know each other. Blockchain removes geographical and social barriers.

## Why Blockchain?

### Immutability
Once rules are set in a smart contract, they cannot be changed. This guarantees fairness for all participants.

### Transparency
All transactions are recorded on the blockchain and can be verified by anyone.

### Automation
Smart contracts automatically execute payments, prize distributions, and collateral seizures without human intervention.

### Global Access
Arisan is no longer limited to physical environments. Indonesian diaspora worldwide can join arisan with family back home.
